FAQ

Common Questions

Is there any downtime after treatments? ▼

Most treatments have zero to minimal downtime. HydraFacial and chemical peels may cause mild redness for 24-48 hours. Laser treatments typically require 2-3 days of recovery.

How many sessions will I need? ▼

Results vary by treatment and skin concern. HydraFacial shows immediate results. Laser and chemical peels typically need 3-6 sessions for optimal results. We'll create a customized plan during your consultation.

Do you accept insurance? ▼

We accept HSA/FSA cards and offer financing through CareCredit. Most treatments are considered cosmetic and not covered by insurance, but we offer flexible payment plans.
